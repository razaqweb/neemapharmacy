About

The Vision of Neema Pharmacy is to provide professional Pharmaceutical Consultation and simple access to quality medication. However, we also aim to utilize the abundance of natural resources in Zanzibar, to produce and distribute locally made pharmaceutical products and cosmetics at affordable prices.
— Abdul Mzale | Founder & Managing Director

Skinema

Skinema is a brand of locally sourced and produced cosmetics. With a focus on natural ingredients and no artificial coloring.

All products are made in the Neema Pharmacy Lab, and available at Neema Pharmacy.
